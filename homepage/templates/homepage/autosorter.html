AutoSorter

This project was originally created with the intent of solving the problem of disorganization in digital learning and more specifically students having a messy digital workspace like Google Drive. After further development, we have realized this is a common problem and we broadened our scope. This can now be used by anyone that has many files in their google drive and wants to organize those files into folders. All it takes is authorization of your google account and selection of destination folders. Then, enjoy a neat workspace!
