About us

Who we are

Starting in 2021, our organization aims to bring student-created projects to our community. All of these projects are based on or are expansions of ideas that have come from previous hackathons. We want to give other students around the world an opportunity to publish and make their projects available to real-time customers. These projects have been created by passionate and hardworking students with a common goal of providing value.

Our Values

Our core values revolve around authenticity, drive, care, respect, and integrity. If you have any questions please contact us.
